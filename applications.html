Agricultural Plasma Treatment

Cold plasma enhances agricultural productivity by stimulating seed germination through plasma-induced modification of seed coat permeability and activation of phytohormonal pathways. It also extends shelf life by inactivating microbes on produce surfaces via reactive oxygen and nitrogen species, thus reducing post-harvest losses and improving produce longevity.

Plasma Surface Sterilization

Plasma sterilization is effective in medical and industrial settings for the decontamination of instruments and surfaces, particularly those sensitive to heat and moisture. This non-contact chemical-free method delivers rapid, efficient sterilization while preserving the integrity of materials, making it a safe, fast, and highly effective disinfection solution.
