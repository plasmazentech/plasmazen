About PlasmaZen

PlasmaZen is a deep-tech startup at the forefront of Non-Thermal Plasma technology. We engineer specialised plasma instruments that drive innovation across agriculture and surface sterilization.

Meet the Core Team

Kamalesh Khanna

Co-Founder and CEO

An NIT Trichy alumnus with a B.Tech. in Computer Science Engineering, Kamalesh blends leadership and operational acumen. His experience as School and College President established a strong foundation for strategic initiatives, while his role as a political consultant refined his ability to navigate complex political landscapes.

Shubhabroto Mukherjee

Co-Founder and CTO

Born into a family of plasma physicists, Shubhabroto gravitated toward the world of plasma from an early age. His passion led him to study Electrical and Electronics Engineering at NIT Trichy. Founding PlasmaZen is the realization of his vision to bring plasma technology from the lab to real-world applications for a more sustainable future.

Behind the Scenes

PlasmaZen's innovations arise from the close collaboration of experts in plasma physics, materials science, power electronics, embedded control systems, surface electrochemistry, and fluid dynamics
